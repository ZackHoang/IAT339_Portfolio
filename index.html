About

My name is Viet Anh Hoang, but call me Zack! I am currently a third-year student studying at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT) at Simon Fraser University. Focusing on full-stack web development, I want to bring out elegant, easy to follow solutions and workflow for developing both front-end sites with React as well as back-end servers using ExpressJS and PostgreSQL. As a primarily self-taught developer who has mastered the fundamentals, I also love making small open sources contribution that could help others better experience their application.
